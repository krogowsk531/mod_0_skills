Employee

Attributes:

name (string)
hoursWorked (integer)
employeeOfTheMonth (boolean)
tipsMade (float)

Methods:

changeName (changes the name attribute)
changeHoursWorked (changes the hoursWorked attribute)
isEmployeeOfTheMonth (returns employeeOfTheMonth as true or false)
getTipsMadePerHour (returns tipsMade / hoursWorked)
